SMS Deeplinks

This is how to format deeplink URLs for iOS and Android.

  • Start the url with sms:{PHONE}
  • Enter &body param for iOS followed by body text
  • Enter ?body param for Android followed by body text
Device URL
iOS - Deeplink sms:1-415-843-1652&body=Hello World
Android - Deeplink sms:1-415-843-1652?body=Hello World
